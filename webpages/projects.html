CEO turnover and cultural change

[Work in Progress]

Do CEO turnovers affect company culture? In this research project, I am analyzing the impact of CEO turnovers on employees' perception of company culture using Glassdoor company review data of 1000+ public companies in the United States between 2009 and 2019. I plan to employ text analysis to detect changes in cultural topics of employee reviews during the two years before and after a CEO turnover. To control for confounding factors, I plan to conduct 1-1 matching between companies that experienced CEO turnovers (treatment group) and those that did not (control group) during the same time period.

The project will further explore how factors including incoming CEO background (promoted internally or appointed externally) and company size affect the magnitude and speed of cultural change as a result of CEO turnovers.

Speaker Recognition System

[Python Code]

As my final project for the Python Programming Course at UChicago, I built a speech recognition program using Markov models. Given 3 text files (Obama_speech.txt, Romney_speech.txt, unknown_speech.txt, for example), the speech recognition program is able to predict whether the first or the second speaker is more likely to be the author of the unknown speech.
