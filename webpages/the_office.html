Network Analysis of The Office (US) Characters

[Python Code]

Introduction

Recently, I found The Office (US) transcript dataset from Kaggle, which gave me an opportunity to explore the network relationship among The Office characters through the 9 seasons.

Network Analysis by Seasons

The following graph shows the network of characters from The Office that appear in at least 500 scenes. Each point (node) represents a character. Each line (edge) between two nodes indicates that the two characters have appeared in the same scene. The width of the edge presents the number of times that the two characters shared a scene. The graph applies force-directed graph drawing, which means that characters who have more shared scenes with other characters (greater centrality) are more likely to appear at the center of the graph.

Observations from Season 1 Graphs

  • Jim-Dwight-Pam-Michael are at the center of graph.
  • The Angela-Oscar-Kevin triangle forms the accounting department.
  • Creed does not have a single line in scenes of Season 1.
  • Kelly only has 3 scenes in Season 1.
